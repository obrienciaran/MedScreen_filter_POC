A fake disease meets an evidence filter

Researchers invented a fake disease called "Bixonimania" to see whether fabricated medical claims would propagate into AI systems and their training data. MedScreen is an evidence-grounded truth filter. It scores a paper on the evidence retrieval can actually find, not on how convincing it reads. Here it catches the fabricated disease before it could reach a training set.

UNGROUNDED → REVIEW The claims that assert the disease as a real entity returned zero evidence across the entire live corpus. With no corroboration in the literature, MedScreen flags the paper for review.
